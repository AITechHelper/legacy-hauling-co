FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time

A few of the things folks ask before they book. Don't see your question? Just give us a call.

How much does junk removal cost?▾
Pricing depends on the volume and type of items being hauled. That's why we offer free, no-obligation on-site quotes — we'll take a look and give you a fair, upfront price before any work begins.
How fast can you come out?▾
Often the very next day. We pride ourselves on fast response and great communication — call or text us and we'll get you scheduled as quickly as possible.
What do you take?▾
Furniture, appliances, household junk, yard and tree debris, construction scrap, storage-unit contents, and more. If you're unsure about a specific item, just ask.
Do I need to move everything outside?▾
Nope. We do all the heavy lifting and loading for you. Point to what needs to go and we'll handle it from there — including clean-up afterward.
Do you work with property managers?▾
Absolutely. We regularly help property managers and landlords with rental turns and clean-outs, clearing left-behind items fast so you can get the unit ready.
What areas do you serve?▾
Tulsa and the surrounding metro — Broken Arrow, Owasso, Bixby, Jenks, Sand Springs, Sapulpa, Wagoner, Catoosa, Claremore, Glenpool, Coweta and more. Not sure if we reach you? Give us a call.
Do you handle commercial jobs?▾
Yes — offices, retail spaces, and job-site cleanups are all in our wheelhouse. We'll work around your schedule to keep your operations running smoothly.
How do I book?▾
